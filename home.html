About Brightwood Academy

Founded in 1985, Brightwood Academy has been at the forefront of innovative education for over three decades.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and challenging educational experience that promotes academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. We believe in fostering a love of learning that lasts a lifetime.

With a student-teacher ratio of 12:1, we ensure personalized attention and support for every student's unique learning journey.
